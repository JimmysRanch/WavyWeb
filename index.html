Frequently Asked Questions

Is my dealership eligible even if I have done this before?

Yes. State franchise laws entitle dealers to receive retail rates for warranty work, provided the proper submission process is followed. Typically dealers may submit once per calendar year or on an anniversary date, depending on state law.

Will my team be burdened and have to spend their own time working on this?

No. We handle the professional package completion and direct manufacturer submission so your team can stay focused on day-to-day operations.

Should I focus on Parts or labor first?

Both. We specialize in both parts and labor warranty reimbursement submissions. That's ALL We Do.

How long will the process take or when will I receive my increase?

Typically in 30-60 Days.
